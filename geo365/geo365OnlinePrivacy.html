Geo365 Privacy document.

We take your privacy seriously, but we do store a small amount of configuration information in a file you can find in geo365assets library in order for the system to work. We do not store any of the information you enter while using our app.

The web site you are reading now also uses cookies for analytics and user preferences, but does not store any personal information.

We may change our policy in the future. If so, this page will be updated with the changes.
